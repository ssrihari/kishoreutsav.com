Topics for 'Krishna Vaibhava' Essay writing competitions

5th to 8th standard
  • Krishna, the best friend.
  • Krishna, the best dancer.
  • Kamsa, the villain.
9th to 10th standard
  • Duryodhana, the best friend
  • Krishna, the best teacher
  • Vithura, the best minister
Other notes
  • The time allotted is 45 minutes.
  • All the writing should be inline with the Ithihasa / epic. No satire or criticism will be acceptable.
  • Judges and the Organisers reserve the right to accept or reject any entries / Essays and their decision shall be final.
  • In case of more extraordinary essays are received, the winner will be decided by an extempore speech, if necessary.
  • There is no constraint on number of prizes.
  • These essays will have to be hand written on a ruled paper with name, parent's name, school and class details.
